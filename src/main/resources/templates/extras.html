If you're feeling hungry, there are plenty of options. Dine at one of their six delicious onsite bars and restaurants.

Universal Studios and Islands of adventure are two theme parks that are right next to each other in the heart of Orlando. They both feature a number of rides, roller coasters, and shows. They both feature a section of The Wizarding World of Harry Potter. Islands of Adventure houses Hogsmeade, which has multiple rides, stores, and themed restaurants, while Universal houses Diagon Alley. Just like in the book, if you don't keep your eye out for Diagon Ally, you could miss it. Although the two lands are in separate parks, you can get from one to the other by using the Hogwarts Express; a train ride that travels exclusively between the two Harry Potter sections of the park.

In addition to the two parks, you can visit Citywalk. This is an area similar to Disney Springs in that there's shopping, movies, restaurants, bars, etc... Citywalk is the connection between the two parks.
