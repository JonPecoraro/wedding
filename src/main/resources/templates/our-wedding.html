Our Story

Ashley and Jon met on Match. Jon sent Ashley a peculiar, but authentic first message "Hey Ashley, I see you're a Catholic too. Which church do you go to?" Ashley quickly responded, and that was the beginning of a beautiful thing. After about a month of talking, they finally went on their first date. It was a nice dinner with hours of exhilarating conversation where they were able to connect on many different levels. From their family history in Cincinnati, to their love for cruising, they had a lot more in common than they thought. As time went on, the bond between them grew stronger and their love grew deeper.

Ashley always dreamed of a Disney fairytale proposal. Jon coordinated with Ashley's sister and her parents for an extravagant Disney proposal to make all her dreams come true. They had a wonderful day at Magic Kingdom, followed by an elegant dinner at the California Grill; a restaurant with a view of Cinderella's castle. Then Jon surprised Ashley with an intimate balcony setting for the proposal. Ashley's sister was hiding with a camera, Jon's dad and Ashley's brother-in-law were in the parking lot getting a different angle. Jon proposed on the balcony minutes before the fireworks over Cinderella's castle. Ashley was ecstatic and after a few tears, she said YES!!! They went from the balcony to a bar where unbeknownst to Ashley, her parents and grandparents were waiting. The room was filled with excitement and joy as everyone celebrated the newly engaged couple.
